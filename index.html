Our Specials

Greek Salad

Greek salad, sounds absolutely delicious!
This vibrant dish features a mix of crispy lettuce, colorful peppers, and briny olives, all brought together with Chicago-style feta cheese and crunchy garlic and rosemary croutons. The feta adds a creamy, tangy element that perfectly complements the fresh vegetables.

From US$69

French Fried Potatoes

French fried potatoes, commonly known as French fries, are a beloved snack and side dish enjoyed worldwide.
These crispy, golden delights are made from potatoes that are cut into strips and deep-fried until they achieve a perfect balance of a crunchy exterior and a fluffy interior.

From US$39

American Sausages

Grilled sausage with red bell peppers and onions.
The sausage is browned with grill marks, indicating it has been cooked on high heat. The red bell peppers are cut into strips and have a charred appearance, suggesting they were grilled as well. The onions are deeply caramelized, giving them a rich, sweet flavor.

From US$59
